\section{System Planning}
This chapter summarizes requirement collection and a project timeline.

\subsection{Requirement Analysis and Data Gathering}
Requirements were derived from three sources: analysis of common PDF utility workflows, review of limitations in upload-based tools, and practical constraints of browser-based implementation.

The analysis focused on user tasks that are frequent and high-value in academic and office contexts, such as page merge, split, reordering, and document information extraction.
